I completed my PhD thesis research at Stanford University under Dr. Lingyin Li, professor in the Biochemistry Department at Stanford School of Medicine and a Stanford ChEM-H Institute fellow. My research focused on elucidating the crystal structure of the innate immune checkpoint molecule ectonucleotide pyrophosphatase/phosphodiesterase 1 using x-ray crystallography.
